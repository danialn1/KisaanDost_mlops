About KisaanDost

KisaanDost is a project solely created to facilitate farmers, where they can get answers to their queries as per the latest farming approaches and trends. The idea is to provide an encrypted live chatbot so farmers can type in or speak out their questions and get immediate responses that are proven to be accurate by AI models. The responses will be in the language (English/Urdu), preselected by the farmer, and will be based on factual information. As speech is a natural medium for communication, the idea of a voice-powered chat will allow easy querying of useful information. KisaanDost would help to enable many farmers to ask their questions and get answers in our local language. A chatbot of this nature that answers open-ended questions will be the first of its kind in Pakistan. Moreover, our system would suggest to them some new techniques like using drones for irrigating the crops which would definitely increase the production to a greater extent.
